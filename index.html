Hi!

Foxborugh Regional Charter School is a charter school located in Foxborough, Massachusetts. We have been teaching since 1998. Our school promotes positive ethical, moral, and civic values. We will commit to providing a supportive, professional, and challenging environment for its teachers and staff. We seek to provide students an excellent academic program.

When we first opened in 1998, we served a K-8 population and teached in one building. We have come so far. Over many years, we have grown in enrollment numbers and buildings, and now have an elementary building for grades K-4 and a middle school/high school building from grades 5-12.
